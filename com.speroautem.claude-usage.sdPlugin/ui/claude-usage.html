How do I get my session key?
1Sign in at claude.ai.
2Open DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I) → Application tab.
3Expand Cookies → https://claude.ai.
4Find the cookie named sessionKey and copy its value (starts with sk-ant-sid01-).
5Paste it above and click Test connection.
Your session key is stored only in Stream Deck's settings on this machine — never sent anywhere except claude.ai. It's equivalent to your login, so treat it like a password.
